1. 切換到專案目錄並執行發布指令：

cd "C:\Users\user\Desktop\潤鼓-智慧評估系統\employee_smart_assessment_system\績效後台(數據卡片、報工、機聯網)\潤鼓_後台\app\RunGuManagement" && dotnet publish -c Release -r win-x64 --self-contained true /p:PublishSingleFile=true
